**A. PSD loss computation in generator training section:**

In the `_train_one_epoch` method, in the generator training section (after `generator_loss_wgan = -torch.mean(fake_scores)` and before `generator_loss = generator_loss_wgan`), insert:

```python
        # PSD loss: penalize wrong frequency content
        # Sample real windows for PSD comparison (same batch size as generated)
        psd_indices = torch.randint(0, len(gan_data_list), (self.batch_size,))
        real_for_psd = torch.stack([gan_data_list[idx] for idx in psd_indices]).double()  # (batch_size, window_length)

        # Compute log-PSD for real and fake (log1p protects against log(0))
        real_psd = torch.log1p(torch.abs(torch.fft.rfft(real_for_psd, dim=1)).pow(2).mean(dim=0))
        fake_psd = torch.log1p(torch.abs(torch.fft.rfft(generated_samples, dim=1)).pow(2).mean(dim=0))

        psd_loss = torch.nn.functional.mse_loss(fake_psd, real_psd)
```
